What is Edge?

Edge is a personal effort to bring web application development up to par with other notable platforms. For example, Edge is highly inspired by both Express (for node.js) and the Play!Framework for Java. Its goal is to enable developers to quickly build application, while leveraging the benefits of the Vert.x platform.

How do I get started?

Simply go to our Download section and download the entire framework. Then read our learning materials to get started.
